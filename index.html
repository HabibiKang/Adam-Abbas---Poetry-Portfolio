A Wandering Man's Cologne

"A strong, bittersweet fragrance
I reek of.
A grin of charisma
Above a melting heart.
Supple like butter,
Bold as whiskey."
- A.A

Loose Friction

"The pen is loose;
Slippery
as is the tongue
of the earnest and the
tender.
Though,
so is that of the terribly
honest.
The result of both avenues of
voice dispensation is a
tattoo that remains
permanent.
Unless it is clouded over
by a thick smoke;
overlapping like a
blinding eclipse."
- A.A

Frostbitten

"Embers dance shapelessly;
involuntarily
Candlewicks simmer in flames
incessantly.
Though,
the living is frozen in the ice of
decay.
Upon refusal to depart
Such is the man
who dreadfully
obeys."
- A.A

Hesitant Risks

"Are all gambles just
wingless flings of
head rushes and
heart gallops?
Are they this in denial of
being
implausible efforts?
Or are some
Feats of triumphant
endurance?
Stretching out an extension
of their innermost
Worthwhile
Endeavors?"
- A.A
