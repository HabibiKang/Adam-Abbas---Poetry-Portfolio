Weekly Poems

The Greed of Need

"Greed is man's ultimate demise.
But fortune,
that is what blinds.
Though, the blind believe
it is they;
themselves,
who are wise.
Oh, the consequence of believing
lies.
What a pleasant
surprise
contentedness is to those
blessed with
fortunate
eyes"
- A.A

Conniving Compassion

"Exuberantly distasteful,
such is the muck of a
fake smile.
Or a double-edged
cheer."
- A.A

The Covetous

"The covetous possess
Everything.
But, they have
Nothing.
Though they seek to
attain
more in quantity
they are dehydrating to
death.
Thirsting for
quality of
experience."
- A.A

Ashes

"Ashes arise from a
continual burning.
Hence why they are
taken
by the finest graze
of wind.
Mind you;
a gust."
- A.A

A Good Song

"A good song
is a
wandering
soul."
- A.A

The Stench of Envy

"The stench of the
envious is a
gentle tease.
Though,
so is that of the meek;
the appreciative.
The dividing sword;
the only surefire way to
decipher
between
the two
is to consider how
they made you
feel."
- A.A
